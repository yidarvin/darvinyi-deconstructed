# Irving Penn — build research

## Scope and evidence

This chapter uses the twelve Art Institute of Chicago collection records and public display renditions documented in `sources.md`. The group spans 1947 (*After Dinner Games*) to 1986 (*Mouth (for L'Oréal)*), moving among fashion, portraiture, still life, and the travel photographs. The source records also distinguish image dates from later print dates: *Cuzco Children* is dated 1948 and recorded as printed December 1976; the two 1950 fashion photographs are recorded as printed in 1979; *Georgia O'Keeffe (B)* is dated January 31, 1948 and recorded as printed September 1991; *Mouth* is dated 1986 and recorded as printed 1992. These are catalog facts, not inferred production history.

## Technique and printing

The supplied records establish Penn's use of deliberate studio staging across the selected fashion, portrait, and still-life images, and the travel works retain the same controlled frontal arrangement. This is a visual and curatorial description of the selected pictures, not a claim that one technical process was used throughout them. `sources.md` supplies dates and, for several works, print dates, but does not identify a negative process, paper, developer, lighting setup, or darkroom method for each individual image. This chapter therefore discusses visible tonal separation, framing, props, and pose rather than inventing technical production details.

## Camera and exposure information

The ingested manifest contains no EXIF data, and the cited Art Institute records in `sources.md` do not supply a camera body, lens, focal length, aperture, shutter speed, film stock, or exposure for these twelve photographs. No such settings are asserted in the chapter.

## Analyzer observations for the supplied web reproductions

The deterministic analysis is evidence about these ingested display files, not a claim about Penn's original negatives or prints. The largest measured edge densities are *Truman Capote* (0.1074), *Colette* (0.0917), and *Three Asaro Mud Men* (0.0794), consistent with their dense facial, fabric, or spear detail. *Georgia O'Keeffe* (0.0111) and *Rochas Mermaid Dress* (0.0130) are much quieter. Representative extracted palettes are warm gray `#58514a` in *Cuzco Children*, near-black `#1c1917` and cream `#eeece0` in *Harlequin Dress*, and yellow `#ce9e32` with brown-red `#3f1c14` in *Still Life with Triangle and Eraser*.
