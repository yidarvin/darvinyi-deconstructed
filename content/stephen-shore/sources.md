# Stephen Shore — source record

## Selection and acquisition note

This source set concentrates on Shore's 1973–75 road photographs, particularly
the large-format views associated with *Uncommon Places*. The Art Institute of
Chicago provides publicly reachable collection records and direct IIIF image
renditions for each work below. Each downloaded rendition opened successfully
and measures 843px on its long edge: above the 640px normal floor, with no
upscaling or alteration.

All ten works remain copyrighted. The collection pages identify Stephen Shore
as creator; MoMA's Shore collection records carry the notice **Copyright ©
Stephen Shore**, and 303 Gallery represents the artist. Each use below is an
individual **fair-use** assessment: (1) the purpose is nonprofit criticism,
scholarship, and transformative composition teaching; (2) the photographs are
published creative works, but the analysis requires their visual form; (3) the
complete frame is used only because cropping would defeat the stated spatial
lesson, and the downloaded copy is web-sized; and (4) the chapter credits and
links to the collecting museum and neither replaces the museum record, an
original print, nor a licensed reproduction.
