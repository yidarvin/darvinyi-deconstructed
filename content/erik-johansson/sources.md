# Erik Johansson — source record

## Selection and rights note

This is a 12-image cross-section of Johansson's best-known photographic composites,
from the early road illusion to the architectural, landscape, and figure-scale
paradoxes that define the later work. The artist's own [featured gallery](https://www.erikjo.com/featured)
labels this set of works with titles and years; the [portfolio](https://www.erikjo.com/work)
also lists the works as personal projects. Every file below was retrieved directly
from the artist's publicly reachable gallery CDN, opened successfully after download,
and measures 1,500px on its long edge. Files have not been upscaled or altered.

Johansson's [print shop](https://www.erikjo.com/medium-prints) sells signed,
numbered limited editions, including works in this selection. The gallery display is
therefore not an open licence. Each item below is a documented **fair-use**
assessment for this nonprofit criticism, scholarship, and transformative composition
teaching book. Credit and known rightsholder notice: **Erik Johansson © Erik
Johansson; all rights reserved unless the artist states otherwise.** The sourced
renditions are web-sized reference copies; this project credits and links to the
artist's gallery and does not substitute for an original, a signed edition, or a
licensed reproduction.
