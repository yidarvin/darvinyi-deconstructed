# Ruud van Empel — source record

## Selection and acquisition note

This ten-image set traces the signature child portraits and the later, panoramic
constructed landscapes: it gives the builder a usable range of frontal group
portraiture, immersive vegetation, and theatrical horizontal staging. The artist's
[official works archive](https://ruudvanempel.nl/works/) provides the public display
JPEGs and work pages used below; each file was retrieved as the public rendition,
opened locally, and verified at 840–2,000px on its long edge. No file was upscaled,
retouched, or otherwise altered.

The artist's site states that its images are subject to copyright and identifies
Ruud van Empel as a Pictoright member. These are therefore not open-licence files.
Every use below is **fair-use**, assessed for this criticism, scholarship, and
transformative composition-teaching book: (1) the purpose is analytical rather than
decorative; (2) each is a published, highly creative photographic collage, which
weighs against use; (3) the complete but web-sized public display frame is necessary
to teach the stated spatial relationship, and no larger or additional material is
used; and (4) each copy is credited and linked to its artist record and does not
replace a gallery/museum record, an original print, or a licensed reproduction.

     portrait symmetry and proliferating detail.

   - **Use basis: fair-use.** Credit: © Ruud van Empel / Pictoright; artist's official
     public display. The complete portrait and surrounding fabricated ecosystem are
     needed to analyze centered attention, edge density, and constructed depth; this
     credited 857 × 1,200px study is limited, transformative, and non-substitutive.
