# Ruud van Empel — research note

## What the supplied record establishes

The source record identifies a 2006–2024 selection from van Empel's *World*,
*Moon*, *Dawn*, *Generation*, *Theatre*, *Voyage Pittoresque*, *Floresta Negra*,
and *Floresta* series. Its selection note describes the work as constructed from
separately photographed fragments; the recurring child portraits, botanical
tableaux, and panoramic landscape fields are therefore treated here as assembled
photographic images, not as unmodified views of a single place. The record's
official artist links establish the titles and public web renditions, while the
named gallery, auction, and museum records corroborate individual dates and, for
some works, edition or medium information. See [sources.md](sources.md).

## Technique, era, and print information

The ten works span the artist's mature digital-image practice from 2006 to 2024.
The available evidence supports discussion of composited photographic fragments
and visibly constructed botanical/portrait spaces. It does not establish a camera
body, lens, focal length, exposure, ISO, capture format, retouching software,
printer, paper, dimensions, or edition for every work. The ingest manifest records
no capture EXIF: these are web-display JPEGs rather than camera originals or print
documentation. Consequently, the chapter names no camera settings or print process
not specifically documented in the source record.
