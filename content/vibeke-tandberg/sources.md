# Vibeke Tandberg — source notes

This twelve-image research set traces Tandberg's staged selves: the early digital
double, the repeated bridal persona, and later transformations of body and mask. All
twelve local JPEGs opened successfully after acquisition. The shortest long edge is
1,200px; no file was upscaled or altered.

All works remain copyrighted. Kode's exhibition record identifies its works as

on the public artist page. Every entry below is a separate **fair-use** assessment,
not a claim of open access. For each: (1) use is criticism, scholarship, and
