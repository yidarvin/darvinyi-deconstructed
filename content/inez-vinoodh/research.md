# Inez & Vinoodh — research note

## What the supplied record establishes

The supplied source record places this eight-image selection across Inez van
Lamsweerde and Vinoodh Matadin's early digitally altered body image and later
fashion/editorial practice. Kunstmuseum Den Haag describes the pair as pioneers
of digital image-making in its *Can Love Be a Photograph: 40 years of Inez &
Vinoodh* retrospective. The record identifies *Thank You Thighmaster: Britt*
as a 1993 work by Inez van Lamsweerde; the linked Museum Boijmans collection
record corroborates the 1993 digitally modified-nude series. It identifies
*Think Love* as 2025 and *Rihanna and A$AP Rocky* as 2023. The remaining
display files have deliberately bracketed descriptive titles because the
museum carousel does not supply individual catalogue titles or dates. See
[sources.md](sources.md) for the per-image source and fair-use rationale.

## Technique, era, and print information

The record supports discussion of digital intervention in the early altered
nudes and of the duo's staged fashion/editorial images, but it does not
document the method or software used for each individual image. The 2025
*Think Love* caption specifically says it was shot on an Apple iPhone 17 Pro
Max. Beyond that one statement, the sources do not establish camera body,
lens, focal length, aperture, shutter speed, ISO, capture format, lighting
setup, printer, paper, edition, or print process for this web-display set.
The ingest manifest contains no capture EXIF, as expected for the downloaded
display JPEGs. The chapter therefore treats the overlays as evidence about
the finished frames, not as evidence about unrecorded production settings.
