# Image sources — Annie Leibovitz

All eleven source files are public, web-sized display renditions supplied by Sotheby’s on the linked public lot or catalogue pages. They are not open licensed. Each is retained for the chapter’s criticism, scholarship, and transformative composition teaching only; the chapter will credit and link to the relevant record and will not offer a substitute for a print, a museum record, or a licensed reproduction.
