# JeeYoung Lee — research notes

## Verified working method

The source record identifies these photographs as works from *Stage of Mind*, made between 2008 and 2016. Lee hand-builds the room-scale elements of each psychological landscape, places a model in the completed installation, photographs the intact scene, then dismantles it. K11 Art Foundation likewise describes painstakingly fabricated symbolic environments and states that the scenes receive no ulterior photographic alteration. The compositions therefore derive their impossible spaces from physical staging, props, color, and viewpoint rather than digital compositing. [Source shortlist](sources.md)

## Print and photographic record

LensCulture identifies the works as pigment prints and records the stated dimensions for each selected work, from 96 × 120cm to 127 × 217cm. It does not identify a camera body, lens, focal length, aperture, shutter speed, film or sensor, ISO, lighting setup, or original capture format. The supplied 1000px JPEGs are public web-display derivatives and their empty EXIF records cannot establish original camera or print-process details beyond the pigment-print medium stated in the source record.
