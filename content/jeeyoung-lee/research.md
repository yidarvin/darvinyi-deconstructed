# JeeYoung Lee — research notes

## Verified working method

The source record identifies these photographs as works from *Stage of Mind*, made between 2008 and 2016. LensCulture describes Lee hand-building the room-scale elements of each psychological landscape, placing a model in the completed installation, photographing the intact scene, and then dismantling it. The chapter’s compositional claims therefore concern the visible physical staging, props, color, scale, and viewpoint. The available record does not establish post-capture processing, so this chapter makes no claim about it. [Source shortlist](sources.md)

## Print and photographic record

LensCulture identifies the works as pigment prints and records the stated dimensions for each selected work, from 96 × 120cm to 127 × 217cm. It does not identify a camera body, lens, focal length, aperture, shutter speed, film or sensor, ISO, lighting setup, or original capture format. Nine supplied 1000px JPEGs have empty EXIF records; `06-my-chemical-romance.jpg` retains Phase One IQ160, 55mm, 1/4s, f/16, ISO 100, and timestamp metadata. Because this is a public web-display derivative rather than an original-capture file, those retained values are not treated as verified original-capture settings or provenance.
