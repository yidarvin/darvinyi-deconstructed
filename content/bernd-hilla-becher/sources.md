# Bernd & Hilla Becher — source record

## Selection and acquisition note

This source pass selects ten publicly displayed collection reproductions rather
than treating the duo's industrial subjects as interchangeable examples. Together
they establish the Bechers' central teaching problem: a neutral, full-frame view
becomes most legible when comparison turns repeated architecture into a typology.
The first three are public Art Institute of Chicago IIIF display renditions; the
remaining seven are publicly served SFMOMA collection-display renditions. Every
file opened after download and clears the 640px long-edge floor (the delivered
range is 843–5,085px); none was upscaled or altered.

All ten records concern works still under copyright. The Museum of Modern Art and
the Metropolitan Museum of Art identify the applicable notice as **© Estate Bernd
& Hilla Becher** (the Met additionally identifies representation by Max Becher).
The collecting institutions' public record and direct display image are retained
below; use is therefore documented as **fair-use**, not as open access.
