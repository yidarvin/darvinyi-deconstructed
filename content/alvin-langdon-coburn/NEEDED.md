# Alvin Langdon Coburn — source recovery notes

## Vortograph (1917)

The canonical Vortograph was checked at the Art Institute of Chicago (object 1987.379; API record `70745`) and at MoMA. The Art Institute provides an object image but does **not** identify the work as public domain; MoMA identifies copyright as George Eastman House. Neither route yielded a lawfully reusable, downloadable high-resolution source under this sourcing pass.

The University of Michigan VRC record has a 1687 × 2143 rendition, but access is restricted to U-M users, so it was not retrieved. The legal next retry route is a rights-marked open institutional rendition—first the Nelson-Atkins Vortograph record, then any future Getty/Commons mirror that explicitly states reuse rights. No access controls were bypassed and no substitute image was made.

This is not a coverage blocker: twelve verified LOC masters (including one reserve) are locally available, all with long edges of at least 1492px.
