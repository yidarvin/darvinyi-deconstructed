# Tom Chambers — source shortlist

This set samples twelve compositional pivots from Chambers’s publicly displayed portfolio: the early Mexican-reverie, ex-voto, and adolescent-narrative work; the later environmental series; and the recent portrait and allegorical work. The artist’s portfolio identifies the images as photomontages and states that every element is photographed or hand-drawn by Chambers; it also offers the full series sequence and public web display files. All twelve files below were retrieved directly from that public portfolio and decoded successfully as JPEGs. Their long edges are 864–1500px, above the project’s 640px normal acquisition floor.

**Rights basis:** **fair-use** for every selection. Tom Chambers is the artist and copyright holder (the portfolio has no open licence or public-domain dedication). Each item is a separate, limited assessment: this nonprofit book uses a credited, linked, web-sized complete frame for transformative criticism, scholarship, and composition teaching; the highly creative photomontage receives the ordinary protection due a creative work; the whole published frame is necessary to analyze its spatial and narrative construction; and the public-display copy neither replaces a fine-art print, the artist’s portfolio, nor a licensed reproduction. The source pages and the artist’s print/licensing contact route remain linked below.
