# Kirsty Mitchell — source shortlist

Kirsty Mitchell’s official [Wonderland archive](https://www.kirstymitchell.art/wonderland/artworks) identifies these works as part of the 2009–2014 series and supplies title, date, edition information, and public display JPEGs. The site’s copyright line credits `© Kirsty Mitchell`; the works are also offered there as signed, limited-edition prints, so no open licence is claimed.

**Rights basis:** **fair-use** for every selection below. Each is a separate U.S. fair-use assessment: (1) this nonprofit book uses the work transformatively for criticism, scholarship, and composition teaching; (2) each is a highly creative, constructed photographic artwork; (3) the complete, uncropped frame is necessary to examine the particular spatial route, colour structure, and relation of figure, costume, prop, and landscape; and (4) the credited, linked, web-sized public display copy is not a substitute for the artist’s print, artwork page, or licensed reproduction. The JPEGs were served directly from the artist’s public archive without authentication or bypass of a technical control.

## Research context

The official [Wonderland project page](https://www.kirstymitchell.art/wonderland/) records that the 74 photographs were made from 2009–2014 and that Mitchell hand-made the costumes and props before assembling them in the English landscape. Its [about page](https://www.kirstymitchell.art/about/) confirms that the scenes were made at true scale, a useful guard against mistaking the constructed theatricality for a purely digital composite.
